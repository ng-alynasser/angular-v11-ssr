Saudi German Development and Investment Company Limited (SAGECO) is a consultancy, advisory and project development company which wasestablished in 1981.

The Company originally came into being as a joint venture between the Governments of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (Ministry of Finance) and the Federal Republic of Germany (DEG , Cologne) to promote cooperation between the Saudi and German private sectors and provide the requisite consultancy services. After expiration of the 25-years' mandatory period of the Joint Venture, the company was privatized.

SAGECO's activities are governed by the Company's philosophy which is, to uphold the values of Integrity, Quality and Professionalism . Its services are carried out in a reliable, professional and cost-effective way.
